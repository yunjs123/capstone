The Project

AtmoDust is a senior design project done by 3 UNCC Undergraduates in The College of Computing and Informatics. Our goal was to make a website that enables users to check air quality from their nearest air sampling location. We use a free public API provided by the EPA to deliever air quality information to users in an informative and easy to understand way.
